`state.json.continuation` is the durable authority for resume state. The continuation architecture separates execution provenance from bounded-resume authority. `gpd --raw resume` reads `state.json.continuation`, execution lineage, and current handoff artifacts, then emits one canonical resume view. Canonical continuation fields define the public resume vocabulary: `active_resume_kind`, `active_resume_origin`, `active_resume_pointer`, `active_bounded_segment`, `derived_execution_head`, `active_resume_result`, `continuity_handoff_file`, `recorded_continuity_handoff_file`, `missing_continuity_handoff_file`, and `resume_candidates`. `.continue-here.md` and `DERIVATION-STATE.md` are context artifacts, not resume authorities.
